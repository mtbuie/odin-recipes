Banana Pudding

Description

A pudding made from layers of pudding, vanilla wafers, and sliced bananas.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cold milk
  • 1 (5ounce) package of instant vanilla pudding mix (May use other types)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) container of frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of vanilla wafers
  • 14 small bananas, sliced or to taste
  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Whisk milk and pudding mix in a bowl for 2 minutes. Then blend in condensed milk until smooth.
  3. Mix in vanilla extract, then slowly mix whipped topping.
  4. Layer the cookies, banana slices, and pudding in a bowl or tray. May layer as many tiems as desired.
  5. Chill in refrigerator until ready to serve. May serve with more crushed wafers on top.
