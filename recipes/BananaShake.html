Banana Milkshake

Description

A simple to make milkshake made out of bananas with no icecream needed.

Ingredients

  • 3 ice cubes
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar, change as desired
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • Blender

Directions

  1. Gather all incredients
  2. Slice bananas
  3. Place ice, milk, banana slices, sugar, and cinnamon into a blender. Mix until smooth.
  4. Chill in refrigerator and enjoy.
